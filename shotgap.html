ShotGap

ShotGap is an action-packed game, that contains different features:
- An endless world for all your needs!
- A variety of characters, called Modules, that offer a cosmetic and a gameplay advantage!
- Challenging enemies that you'll master during your playtime!

Other perks the game offers:
- It's free!
- No wifi is required to play!
- The game is saved locally!
- The game has no forced ads! Meaning that you'll only watch ads if you want to!

Also, this is the first game that we, Pack A Punch, ever published, so we would be very thankful if you downloaded, and happy if you enjoyed it!

We crafted this game thinking on how to make it more pleasant on the customer side, so that you'll have enough content and won't be annoyed!

Hope you'll enjoy!
